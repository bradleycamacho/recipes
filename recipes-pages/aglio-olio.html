Spaghetti aglio e olio

Impress friends and family with spaghetti aglio e olio, a dish greater than the sum of its parts.

Ingredients

  • Box of spaghetti
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • Head of garlic
  • Bunch of parsley

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook the spaghetti according to the box instructions.
  2. Meanwhile, mince all the garlic and simmer it with the entire cup of olive oil until pale brown. Remove from heat.
